about us

A Family owned business with deep rooted traditions. Red River Bakery is well know in the community for its great, gluten-free foods and amazing atmosphere.
Our Mission
The goal of Red River Bakery is to provide the community with flavorful desserts and breads of many varieties, all gluten-free.

Red River Bakery has been around for over a decade and in that time has amassed many techniques and recipes for our amazing foods!

Q&A

Below are some of the most frequently asked questions that we at Red River Bakery have recieved. We use our experience to bring the best food to your dining room table.
Why RED RIVER BAKERY?
Why should I choose Red River Bakery over another bakery in the area?

Red River has been around for over a decade and has not only the best tasting food according to the Baker's Choice Council, but also the widest variety of greaat baked goods.

WHAT DO WE HAVE?
What food does Red River Bakery have to offer?

Red River offers many different types of baked goods. We have bread loaves, cupcakes, and pies.

HOW DELECTABLE ARE YOUR PRODUCTS?
Are your products really as good customers say they are?
