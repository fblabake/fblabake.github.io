about us

A Family owned business with deep rooted traditions. Red River Bakery is well know in the community for its great, gluten-free foods and amazing atmosphere.
Our Mission
The goal of Red River Bakery is to provide the community with flavorful desserts and breads of many varieties, all gluten-free, along with our new amazing dinner items!

Red River Bakery has been around for over a decade and in that time has amassed many techniques and recipes for our amazing foods!

our team

The core of our team is a group of three individuals that are in love with baking and well versed in both gluten-free cooking and baking. We are here to help make your eating experience the greatest it can possibly be!
James is a Nebraska born entrepreneur with a passion for food. He has a gluten intolerance, and through his experience, he has learned to perfect the gluten-free culinary arts. He has been working in the baking industry for 23 years, and has been the owner of Red River Bakery since its inception. He lives in Omaha with his wife and two daughters.
James Griffin

Owner

Marco, a Chicago native, has been interested in cooking all of his life. A renowned chef in his own right, Marco is a fantastic member of our team, with years of experience in both baking bread goods and cooking dinner food items. When he was presented with the new opportunity of learning to cook sans gluten, he jumped at the chance. Marco has relocated to Omaha along with his fiancée.
Marco Romano

Q&A

Below are some of the most frequently asked questions that we at Red River Bakery have received. We use our experience to bring the best food to your dining room table.
Why RED RIVER BAKERY?
Why should I choose Red River Bakery over another bakery in the area?

Red River has been around for over a decade and has not only the best tasting food according to the Baker's Choice Council, but also the widest variety of great baked goods.

WHAT DO YOU HAVE?
What food does Red River Bakery have to offer?

Red River offers many different types of baked goods. We have bread loaves, cupcakes, and pies. Also, we are starting to expand our bakery to include an Italian restaurant.

HOW ENJOYABLE ARE YOUR PRODUCTS?
Are your products really as good as customers say they are?

We have the most appetizing products in the world. We have one of the most famous chefs in the baking business working for us.

HOW DO I EARN REWARDS?
How would I join the loyalty program?

Click Here to register for our loyalty program. After you have joined, you will begin to earn points with every purchase. Every $5 you spend, you earn one point. Every point you earn can be spent as one dollar in store on your next purchase!

WHAT OTHER FOODS DO YOU OFFER?
Other than baked goods, what else does Red River have to offer?

Red River Bakery is starting to offer a selection of Italian foods. We have multiple varieties of a gluten free pasta and over 20 different toppings for our gluten free pizzas.
